About the club

Whimsical, warm, and intentionally unhurried.

This supper club is a standing invitation to pause once a month and gather around a beautiful table. Think spring blooms, handwritten menus, simple but thoughtful food, and a guest list made up of people we already love.

There is no membership, no fee, and no fuss — just a sweet little tradition for friends.

What to expect

A different feeling each month, the same lovely spirit every time.

❀

Seasonal tables

Fresh flowers, layered linens, candlelight, and little details inspired by the season.

☽

Shared meals

A thoughtful menu built around comfort, beauty, and the joy of lingering at the table.

✧

Friendly rhythm

One gathering each month so it feels special, easy to remember, and never overwhelming.
